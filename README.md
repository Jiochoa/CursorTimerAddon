<h2><strong>Overview</strong></h2>
<p><strong>CursorTimer</strong> is a lightweight, high-performance combat utility designed to keep your eyes where they belong: on the action.</p>
<p>Ever missed an ability because you were looking at your action bars to check a cooldown? This addon dynamically mirrors your spell icons and timers directly next to your mouse cursor the moment a spell is on cooldown.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Combat Focus:</strong> No more "tunnel visioning" on your bars.</li>
<li><strong>Intelligent Logic:</strong> Only shows up when needed and auto-hides based on your preference.</li>
<li><strong>Ultra Lightweight:</strong> Written with optimized Lua to ensure zero impact on your FPS.</li>
</ul>

<h2><strong>How-To-Use</strong></h2>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Open Settings:</strong> Type <code>/ct</code> in your chat window to open the Options panel.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Enable Test Mode:</strong> At the top you can <strong>Enable Test Mode</strong>. This allows you to see the icon while you move sliders.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Adjust Layout:</strong> Move the <strong>X and Y Offset</strong> sliders until the icon is at a comfortable distance from your cursor.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Go to Combat:</strong> Once configured, the icon will appear automatically whenever you attempt to use a spell that is on cooldown.</p>
</li>
</ol>
